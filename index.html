About Us

Lady Million by Paco Rabanne is a Floral Fruity fragrance for women. Top notes are Raspberry, Neroli and Amalfi Lemon; middle notes are Jasmine, African Orange flower and Gardenia; base notes are white honey, Patchouli and Amber.
